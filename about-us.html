About Us

Rohan Energy and Transport Limited (ROHAN) is a registered Ghanaian Limited Liability Company (CS 162160821). Our specialization includes advisory services in the downstream energy sector, and providing strategic planning, implementation, leasing, and training services in aviation, ground transportation, and marine transport sectors. Additionally, we offer comprehensive support as a one-stop shop for general business advisory and startup services. At ROHAN, our mission is to ignite sustainable solutions and deliver expertise that spans the realms of energy, transportation, and business."
